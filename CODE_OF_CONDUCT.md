# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1.

## TL;DR

* Be kind and direct.
* Disagree with ideas, not with people.
* Assume the other person is acting in good faith.
* If you experience or witness abuse, email `coc@nerve.dev` — a maintainer
  will respond within 72 hours.

The full text of the Covenant applies; we do not maintain a divergent
version.
